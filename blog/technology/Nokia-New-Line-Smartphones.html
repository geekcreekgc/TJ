Nokia New Line Smartphones


Intro

Nokia, a company that made a name for itself with flexible cell phones in the 1990s, has announced its new version of the smartphones in CES 2022. The Nokia C100, Nokia C200, and the new Nokia G400 are all manufactured by HMD Global.

Credit: NDTV Gadgets 360

Price

Nokia's new line of smartphones costs around $ 50, much less than the Apple or Samsung smartphone. The Nokia G400 costs about $ 100, which is compatible with the iPhone 4s.

Screen Size

The Nokia C100 is a 3.2 inch screen, the Nokia C200 is a 2.8-inch screen, and the Nokia G400 is a 2.8-inch screen.

Credit: 25HTECH.ASIA

Cameras

The Nokia C100, Nokia C200, and Nokia G400 all have 8 megapixel cameras. It can take sharp and clear images and has a lens made of glass and covered with abrasive materials for extra protection. The camera is also equipped with many additional features such as Adobe Photoshop Lightroom features, zoom capability, and 1080p HD video capture capability.

Details

The Nokia C100 has a 3.5-inch display with 240 x 320 resolution, 512 MB RAM, and a microSD card slot slot. The Nokia C200 is equipped with a 5-megapixel camera, 16 GB memory, and a Bluetooth connection. The Nokia G400 has the same features as the C200 but shares the same 2 megapixel camera.

Credit: Sector 55's Youtube Channel

Colors Available

The Nokia C100, Nokia C200, and Nokia G400 are all available in a variety of colors. The Nokia C100 starts at around $ 105, making it a good choice for those on a low budget. The G400 is the most expensive of the three phones, but it has many features that make it worth the extra cost.
