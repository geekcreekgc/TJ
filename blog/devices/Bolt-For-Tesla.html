Bolt For Tesla

Tesla's Bolt: The Electric Car That Goes Half the Price, Twice the Range For 2022, the Chevrolet Bolt EV gets a number of styling revisions and new technologies along with a sizable price cut. Meanwhile, the Tesla Model 3 keeps leading EV sales with unmatched range and breathtaking acceleration.


Section 1: What's the Bolt EV?


The Chevrolet Bolt EV is a fully-electric hatchback available with a 240-mile range and a price tag below $30,000 after tax incentives and rebates in the US and Canada. The $37,495 base model gets a 200-horsepower, 1.5-liter inline four-cylinder engine with a nine-speed automatic transmission and an electric range of up to 238 miles.

Why is the Bolt EV a good alternative to the Model 3?

The Chevrolet Bolt EV has significantly better range than the Model 3, 50 miles more than the smaller sedan. The hybrid GM vehicle can get from 0 to 60 miles per hour in less than 6 seconds. For the 2020 model year, the Bolt EV can go from 0 to 60 mph in as little as 6.5 seconds, making it quicker than the Tesla Model S (5.1 seconds) and Model X (6.0 seconds) by the time those vehicles are on the road.
