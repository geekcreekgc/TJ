Forza Horizon 5 is an upcoming racing game by UK-based developer Playground Games and US-based game publisher Microsoft Studios. It is the fifth entry in the open world racing series Forza Horizon, and it will be released on October 2, 2018. Forza Horizon 5's map will be bigger than any other Forza Horizon game, containing over 350 different types of terrain to race across.

The game features a new mechanic called 'Drivatars', which are AI drivers who are based on real players' driving data from previous games in the series.
