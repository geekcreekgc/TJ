Fornite Chapter 2 Season 8



Everything new in fornite chapter 2 season 8 ------------ Few Changes in the Map, Sideways Weapons, New NPCs, cosmetics and more.

Fornite Chapter 2 Seasons 8 has just gotten started, and with the plethora of chnages and additions made in-game, players are going to have a hard time taking it all in at once.

This article includes all the major changes, additions and tidbits that of information that returning players and newscomers to the games can read to get themselves up to speed with latest happenings.

New Things in FORTNITE CHAPTER 2 SEASON 8 :

1> Maps Changes ----

From the previous seasons there are few changes. According to leakers , there will be two distinct maps for players. One will be feature a normal map with broken pieces of the Mothership, demaged, POIs and the like. While the other map will feature the Sidesways region. In theory, players will be able to visit both of these maps during a match. In addition to the two new maps for Fortnite Chapter 2 Seasons 8, a brand new Lobby Island has been added as well. Small chunks of the Mothership can be seen scattered about.

2> Sideways weapons and crafting -------

The develops have introduced somethings called " Sideways Weapons" in Fornite Chapter 2 Season 8. Based on the information on Epic Games blog, players will be able to enter the Sideways and obtain these powerful weapons. They can also be upgraded during a match. In addition to the sideways weapons, other weapons ; other items such as Port-a-portal, shockwave launchers, symbiotes and even the flight knock pistol have been to the game. Aside from the brand new loot pool, players will also be able to craft a myriad of weapons using old and new crafting materials, which they will be to collect by looting.

5> The Foundation----------------

Previously in season 7 the Spire was destroyed and was tossed into the ocean and never to be seen again. This member of "THE SEVEN " will finally made a comeback appearence in the season 8 story trailer.

6> Punchcard ----------

A fan favorite feature known as Punchcard will finally arrive in season 8 in game. Players can earn vast amount of experience points by completing the challenges associated with them.

7> Wildlife -------------

To accompany the ominous theme of season 8 crows have been added to the game. They are the first flying aniamls to be added as a feature in Fortnite. According to a contant creator HYPEX another larger flying creature will also be added in game, but the timeline of the animal has not been mentioned Previously an animal known " Raptor " was added in game which is the deadliest than any other player in the entire map, which can outrun any player.

8> Miscellaneous----------

A New and inproved version of the mounted turrent has been added to Fortnite Chapter 2 Season 8. Dubbed the Mounted Turrent Mark 2, this fixed weapon will be used by a lot of in-game players in the future. In addition to Mapcap, madness has ensured on the island since the Mothership crashed. Alongside dozens of cubes, a golden one has also landed on the island. At the moment, it's unclear what this new cube varient will do in-game.

9> Fortnite Chapter 2 Season 8 end date ------------

Athough Fortnite Chapter 2 Season 8 has just begun, an end has also been announced. According to the Fortnite content creator HYPEX the season 8 will end on 5th December 2021.
